Why I made this website

I made this website to share nasi lemak locations all over malaysia to fellow malaysians and other people who are just looking to try our national dish.

What is Nasi Lemak?

Nasi Lemak is Malaysia's National dish. Nasi lemak consists of fragrant rice cooked in coconut milk and pandan leaf.

Ingredients

Traditionally, nasi lemak is wrapped and served in banana leaves, added with a hot spicy sauce (sambal) and various garnishes, including cucumber slices, small fried anchovies (ikan bilis), roasted peanuts, and hard-boiled or fried eggs.
